What about me?

As you might guess... I'm David! I'm from Barcelona. I get my degree on Computer Engineering at UPF in 2019. I did the Master in Intelligent Interactive Systems at UPF in the 2020/21 course as well .
I consider myself a proactive and curious guy, which always wants to learn new things. I try to learn from my environment. I also consider myself quite creative, responsible and highly social. I'm not made to work alone, I definetly prefer team working.
I find Artificial Intelligence in general highly interesting, but this interest grows when the goal of a project has direct impact on the society or in persons. Analyzing and predicting social fenomena, getting information from human activities and understanding them from data is what really interests me, like Social Computing, Web Intelligence and Data-Driven Analytics.
Summarizing my work experience, I have mostly worked on web 3D graphics and graph based interfaces, as I've been working on the GTI (Interactive Technologies Group) research group from UPF for 4 years. The last 3 years I've been the main developer of UPF contribution to SAUCE , a H2020 European project. Also, I've been associate professor, teahcing Computer Graphics course. I've also participated in other EU projects. You can see more details on my CV, clicking here.
Leaving a little bit the professional context, I love the adventure. I'm MTB rider (purely hobby, nothing professional) and also advanced diver when the weather allows me to enjoy the sea life without freezing.

Medusa

Medusa is a standalone web based tool which allows to create scenarios with virtual characters, build custom behaviours through graph programming, stylize the body pose of the virtual characters through their emotions, among other features. This tool was developed within the SAUCE project context.

HBTreeJS

Core JavaScript library of Medusa. Implementation of a Hybrid version of Behaviour Trees, as well as the implementation of several graph nodes using as base LiteGraph, which allow to create rich and complex behaviour for the virtual characters. HBTreeJS is exportable to other systems, such a videogames or Behaviour Planners.

What do I mean
with my emoji?

Computational Semantics project, where the goal is to analyze the usage and the perception of the emojis, to check if the perception is correlated with the usage of them. The analysis is approached through four different tasks: computation of the emoji embedding, prediction of an emoji given a particular text, emoji clustering and sentiment prediction comparing texts with and without emojis.
